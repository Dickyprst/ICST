About to ICST 2022

International Conference on Science and Technology (ICST) is a scientific forum to discuss the development and latest research on exact science, engineering and technology. The following topics of third conference are Science and Engineering Physics, Mathematics and statistics, Computer Science, Electricity and Electronics, Disruptive Technologies in the 4th Industrial Revolution (IIoT, Artificial Intelligent, Big Data, AR & VR, Sensors, 3D Printing, Robots, Machine Learning), Geographic Information System & Remote Sensing, Biological & Environmental Science, Biomedics and Biometrics, Geophysic, Geology and Mining, Mechanical Engineering, Industrial Engineering, Civil Engineering. Technology-based applications such as learning media for education can also be accepted at this Conference.
